The Money 20/20 Gold Raffle

Draw: 3 June 2026 at 16:20-16:40 · World Gold Council Booth · Money 20/20 Europe, Amsterdam

1Organiser

The raffle is organised by WGC (UK) Limited (“the Organiser”), a part the World Gold Council The organiser is registered in England & Wales with UK company number 07867682. Its registered address is at 7th Floor, 15 Fetter Lane, London EC4A 1BW, UK.

2Eligibility

Participation in the raffle is open to all attendees of Money 20/20 Europe 2026 who:

  • are aged 18 or over at the time of entry;
  • are physically present at the Money 20/20 Europe conference in Amsterdam on 2-3 June 2026; and
  • complete a valid entry via the digital registration system at the World Gold Council booth.

Employees, contractors, and agents of the World Gold Council and Boston Consulting Group, and their immediate family members, are not eligible to participate.

3How to Enter

Entry is free of charge. There is no purchase necessary to participate.

To enter, participants must complete the digital registration form at the World Gold Council booth, providing their full name, email address, and company or organisation. Participants must also agree to the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Notice prior to submission.

Only one entry is permitted per person. Duplicate entries (identified by email address) will be disregarded.

Registration closes when the Organiser locks the entry system at or before the draw time. No entries will be accepted after registration is closed.

4The Prize

One (1) main winner will receive one (1) Britannia 2026 1oz Gold Bullion Coin, Bullion, 999.9 Fine Gold. Two (2) runner-up winners will each receive one (1) 1g Gold Britannia Minted Bar, 999.9 Fine Gold from The Royal Mint, together the “Prizes”.

A full description of the Prizes, including their estimated retail value, will be displayed at the World Gold Council booth and on the registration screen. The total value of the Prizes does not exceed €4,500.

The Prize is non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash or any other prize. The Organiser reserves the right to substitute the Prize with an alternative of equal or greater value if circumstances outside its reasonable control make this necessary.

5The Draw

The draw will take place on 3 June 2026 at approximately 16:30 at the World Gold Council booth at Money 20/20 Europe, Amsterdam.

The winner will be selected at random from all valid entries using a computerised random selection tool, constituting an impartial determination by chance in accordance with the Dutch Code of Conduct for Promotional Games of Chance 2014.

The draw result will be final and binding. No correspondence will be entered into.

6Winner Notification and Prize Claim

The winner must be physically present at the World Gold Council booth at the time of the draw (3 June 2026 from 16:20-16:40) to claim the Prize. If the selected winner is not present during that time, a new winner will be drawn from the remaining valid entries. This process will be repeated until a present winner is identified.

The winner will be announced at the booth and contacted by email following the event. The Organiser reserves the right to request proof of identity before awarding the Prize.

The Prize will be awarded as promptly as reasonably practicable following the draw. The results of the draw will be communicated within three months of the draw date.

Personal data collected during registration will be processed by the World Gold Council for the purposes of administering the raffle, identifying and contacting the winner, and complying with applicable legal obligations, and in relation to marketing communications where you choose to opt-in to receive them (noting that you may withdraw your consent at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in any marketing communication or by contacting us directly).

For full details of how your data is used, your rights, and how to contact us, please refer to the World Gold Council Privacy Notice, available at: gold.org/privacy.

By participating in the raffle, you confirm that you have read and agree to the terms of the Privacy Notice.
