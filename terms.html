The Britannia Gold Giveaway

Version 1.0 · Last updated April 2026

01The Promoter

The Britannia Gold Giveaway (the “Promotion”) is run by the World Gold Council (“WGC”, “we”, “us”), a not-for-profit company registered in England and Wales, with offices at 7th Floor, 15 Fetter Lane, London EC4A 1BW, United Kingdom.

02Promotion Period

The Promotion opens at 09:00 BST on 3 June 2026 and closes at 16:15 BST on 3 June 2026 (the “Promotion Period”). Entries received outside this window will not be accepted. The live draw will take place at 16:30 BST on 3 June 2026 at the World Gold Council stand at Money 20/20 Europe, RAI Amsterdam.

03Eligibility

To enter, you must:

  1. Be aged 18 years or older at the time of entry;
  2. Be a registered attendee of Money 20/20 Europe 2026;
  3. Be legally resident in a jurisdiction in which it is lawful to receive a prize of physical investment-grade gold without restriction or licence;
  4. Not be an employee, contractor, director, officer or immediate family member of the World Gold Council, The Royal Mint, BullionByPost, or any party professionally connected with the Promotion.

Entries from persons who do not meet these criteria are void. The WGC reserves the right to verify eligibility at any time and to require proof of identity, age and residence before a prize is awarded.

04How to Enter

  1. Complete the entry form at the WGC stand or via the online entry link provided by the WGC;
  2. Provide your full name, email address, country of residence, and accept these Terms & Conditions;
  3. One entry per person. Duplicate, automated, or fraudulent entries will be disqualified.

No purchase is necessary. There is no fee to enter. Internet access and a valid email address are required. The WGC accepts no responsibility for entries that are lost, delayed, corrupted, or not received for any reason.

05The Prizes

Three (3) prizes are available in total:

  • First prize (1 winner): One (1) × 2026 Britannia 1oz Gold Bullion Coin, issued by The Royal Mint (999.9 fine gold, UK legal tender).
  • Runner-up prizes (2 winners): One (1) × 1 gram Britannia Gold Bar each, supplied via BullionByPost (999.9 fine gold, assay-sealed).

Prizes are non-transferable, non-exchangeable, and there is no cash alternative. In the unlikely event that any prize becomes unavailable due to circumstances beyond our reasonable control, we reserve the right to substitute a prize of equal or greater value. The market value of physical gold fluctuates; the WGC makes no representation as to the future value of any prize.

06The Draw

Winners will be selected at random from all valid entries received during the Promotion Period. The draw will be conducted live at the WGC stand at 16:30 BST on 3 June 2026, using an independently verifiable random selection method. The first name drawn will receive the 1oz Gold Britannia Coin; the second and third names drawn will each receive a 1g Gold Bar.

07Notification & Claiming Prizes

  1. Winners present at the live draw may claim their prize on the day, subject to ID verification;
  2. Winners not present will be notified by email within 2 business days of the draw;
  3. A winner must respond and provide a valid postal address within 14 days of notification, failing which the prize will be forfeited and a replacement winner drawn at the WGC's sole discretion;
  4. Prizes will be dispatched by tracked insured courier to the winner's nominated address within 30 days of claim confirmation. Delivery is provided to addresses in the United Kingdom and EU only; winners outside these regions are responsible for any import duties, taxes, or arrangements required to receive the prize.

08Taxes & Personal Responsibility

Winners are solely responsible for any taxes, duties, levies, or other charges that may arise in connection with receiving or owning the prize in their country of residence. The WGC strongly recommends that winners seek independent tax advice. The prize is awarded in physical form only; the WGC does not provide custody, storage, or insurance for the prize after delivery.

09Data Protection

Personal data submitted in connection with the Promotion will be processed in accordance with the WGC Privacy Policy. The legal basis for processing is your consent. Data will be used solely:

  • To administer the Promotion, contact winners, and deliver prizes;
  • To send marketing communications, only where you have explicitly opted in;
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

Personal data will be retained for no longer than is necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, and in any event no longer than 24 months after the end of the Promotion Period (unless required by law). You may withdraw consent, request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing privacy@gold.org.

Photographs taken at the live draw may be used by the WGC for promotional purposes. Winners may opt out of publicity by emailing the address above prior to the draw.
